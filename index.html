You

WASD / arrowsmove — your facing follows your movement
X or TABswitch weapon: stow the rifle, draw the sword (takes a moment)
clickattack with the weapon in hand — the rifle aims at your cursor
Rreload the Martini-Henry (single shot, slow reload)
SPACEsword swing, toward the direction you're facing

Squad orders

Qmake ready — everyone reloads (you too, rifle in hand) and allies brace
EFIRE! — volley; a readied volley shoots tighter and hits harder
1form line — firing line abreast of you; only holds while you stand still
2behind me (default) — allies cover from your back
3fire at will — they hold their current position and keep shooting; wildest aim in the game, self-reloading
4charge — swords out, run the enemy down

Rest at the homestead to heal and rally fallen allies. The deeper east you push, the tougher the enemies — and the rarer the artifacts. Loot counts once you carry it home. After a volley the muskets are empty until you order make ready.
